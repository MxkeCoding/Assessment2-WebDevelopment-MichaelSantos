MOUTHWASHING

The Game

Mouthwashing is a 2024 psychological horror adventure game developed by Wrong Organ and published by Critical Reflex. It is a game set in space, where five crew members are stranded after a crash. As food and supplies run out, they resort to drinking mouthwash, leading to escalating tensions.
